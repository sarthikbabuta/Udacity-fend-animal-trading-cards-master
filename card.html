Interesting Facts About Dogs.There are more than 150 dog breeds, divided into 8 classes: sporting, hound, working, terrier, toy, non-sporting, herding, and miscellaneous.

  • Scientific Name:Canis lupus familiaris
  • Average Length:6.3-110 cm
  • Average Lifespan:10-13 years
  • Habitat:Grasslands

Dogs (Canis lupus familiaris) are domesticated mammals, not natural wild animals. ... They are sometimes called "canines" from the Latin word for dog - canis. Sometimes people also use "dog" to describe other canids, such as wolves. A baby dog is called a pup or puppy.
